About us

Just Eat is a chain of casual restaurants in Bulgaria and Barcelona. Thanks to the exceptionally good food quality and the high standard of service, the Happy Bar & Grill brand is the strongest in its class in Bulgaria, as well as the largest number of restaurants. The Happy Bar & Grill is located in the most central locations in major cities, as well as on the highways and major roads of the country. In addition to the Happy brand, our Company also establishes and develops a number of other brands: SASA Fashion Food, Captain Cook and REZZO.

Just Eat is a service of Happy Bar & Grill, started in 2010, which to date operates in all cities that have Happy restaurants, with the exception of the national road network.

A taste for life and a taste for food are used as synonyms in brand communication.
